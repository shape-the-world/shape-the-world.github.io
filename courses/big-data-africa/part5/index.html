Reconstruction from real data

In this last part the participants apply their modelling pipeline to real data and assess the performance and limitations of the model. They also quantify the uncertainty of their model prediction

Contents​

  • Evaluating a fitted model and comparing multiple models (Slides)
  • Practice: Fit the model to real-data (Jupyter-Notebook)

Learning goals​

The participants

  • can use the developed components to derive a solution that works on real data.
  • know a variety of techniques to assess the properties and limits of their fitted models.
  • can assess the uncertainty inherent in the model.
  • understand the full Bayesian workflow for shape modelling and can apply it to other inference problems.

Reading​

  • Gelman, Andrew, et al. "Bayesian workflow." arXiv preprint arXiv:2011.01808 (2020) (pdf), Pages 30 - 45.
